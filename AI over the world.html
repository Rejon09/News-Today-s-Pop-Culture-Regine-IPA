What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI provides computer programs with the ability to think and learn independently. It is a simulation of human intelligence in machines to perform tasks we typically rely on humans for.

Types of Artificial Intelligence Based on Its Capabilities

There are three main types of AI based on its capabilities: Weak (Narrow) AI: Focuses on a single task and cannot perform beyond its limits (common in our daily lives). Strong (General) AI: Can understand and learn any intellectual task that a human can. Super AI: Surpasses human intelligence and can perform any task better than a human.
